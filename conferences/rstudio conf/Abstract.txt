Life expectancy at birth is an important indicator of overall health of a population. While life expectancy has increased in the United States over time, a black-white life expectancy gap persists, although it has narrowed over time.

In this talk, I will illustrate how I've used shiny to explore state-level trends in the life expectancy gap for males and females.
